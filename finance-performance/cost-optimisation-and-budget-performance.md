# Cost Optimisation & Budget Performance Analysis

## Context
Organizations operating with constrained resources must balance operational effectiveness with financial discipline. Without structured budget monitoring and cost analysis, spending decisions often become reactive rather than strategic.

## Problem
Limited visibility into cost drivers and budget performance reduced the ability to proactively manage expenditures and assess the financial impact of operational decisions.

## Financial Approach
I supported cost control and budget performance through:
- Monitoring budget execution against planned allocations
- Identifying key cost drivers and variance drivers
- Supporting cost optimisation initiatives aligned with operational priorities
- Providing structured financial insights to support decision-making

## Analysis & Reporting
- Tracked budget vs. actual performance on a periodic basis
- Analyzed cost variances and spending trends
- Supported scenario-based thinking for cost reduction and resource allocation
- Prepared structured summaries for management review

## Tools Used
- Excel for budget tracking and variance analysis
- Financial performance metrics and ratio analysis
- Structured reporting templates for management communication

- Clear financial reporting enhances decision quality across functions
